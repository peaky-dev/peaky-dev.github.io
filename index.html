01 — About
Good software, built with care

Peaky Development is a consulting firm based in Malmö. We help companies build, maintain, and improve their digital products — whether that's a web platform, a mobile app, or the infrastructure holding it all together.

We work closely with our clients, embedding in their teams when needed and operating independently when that makes more sense. The goal is always the same: deliver solid work, on time, without the fuss.

We're a small team that values clarity over complexity. We'd rather have an honest conversation about trade-offs than promise the moon. Most of our work comes through referrals, which we take as a good sign.

If you have a project that needs capable hands and level heads, we'd be happy to talk.
